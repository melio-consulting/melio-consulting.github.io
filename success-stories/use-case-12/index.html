Business Context

Women across South Africa often receive lab results filled with jargon and no follow-up support‚Äîleading to anxiety, confusion, and delayed care. Femmflo, a FemTech startup, partnered with Melio AI to launch an AI-powered chatbot that interprets medical PDFs and mental wellness data into personalised, cited responses without needing an internal tech team.


Challenge

The team needed to deliver a working, investor-ready MVP that could:

  • Interpret lab result PDFs and onboarded health data
  • Provide safe, cited medical insights with conversational UX
  • Scale for future generative AI upgrades
  • Launch within 2 months, with no in-house AI or DevOps staff

Solution

AI-enabled health assistant with AWS-native infrastructure

Melio AI delivered a chatbot MVP in 8 weeks using AWS services for agentic workflows, secure file handling, serverless compute, and scalable architecture. Key features included in the Generative AI powered Bedrock agent included:

  • Context-aware answers tailored to onboarding and lab results
  • Bedrock guardrails for safeguarding sensitive health-related advice for users
  • Session memory and follow-up capability
  • Automated fallback messages with cited sources
  • OpenWebUI application hosted using AWS ECS
  • Comprehensive evaluation framework to ensure the solution delivered accurate and relevant responses

The platform was designed for extensibility, with Amazon Bedrock laying the foundation for additional GenAI use cases.


Impact and Results

The MVP helped Femmflo unlock investment conversations and pilot testing. With no internal AI team, the founders were able to launch a safe, low-cost, high-impact health assistant using AWS and Melio AI‚Äôs managed stack.
