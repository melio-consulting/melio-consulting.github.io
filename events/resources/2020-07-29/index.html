CNCJ - July, 2020

Multi-cluster Kubernetes, the easy way [with Rancher]

Summary

We had the pleasure of hosting Tom Otomanski at our July CNCJ meetup last week. Tom showcased how Rancher enables effortless management of multiple Kubernetes clusters through a single interface.

In this session, Tom covered:

  • Creating a Kubernetes cluster on AWS through Rancher
  • Managing Kubernetes cluster with common operations through Rancher
  • Showcased all Kubernetes configurations in the Rancher GUI
