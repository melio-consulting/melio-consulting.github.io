CNCJ - September, 2020

Using Google Anthos to unlock cloud-native hybridization awesomeness

Summary

Google Anthos has come a long way since its GA announcement more than a year ago. Anthos is a combination of many services under the hood - all revolving around easing the deployment of Kubernetes anywhere and everywhere.

Julian has taken us through how a hypothetical SaaS company leverages Anthos to deliver their products to their customers.

In this session, Julian covered:

  • An overview of Google Anthos
  • Migrating workloads to the cloud using Google Anthos
